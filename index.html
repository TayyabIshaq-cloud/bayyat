Eyemazing treatment lasts 10 minutes. We suggest starting with 1 treatment per day, 3 days per week.

If your skin is very sensitive, we suggest reducing it to 5 minutes a day and 2 day a week to avoid redness.

Please avoid treatment if you have been exposed to the sun in the last 24 hours, if you have already reddened skin or have any type of irritation.

What do coloured led's mean?
add

Colored LED'S are used to differentiate treatments, each colored light corresponds to a different function:

When the Blue light blinking: "Red light therapy ON"

When the Blue light is still (not blinking): "EMS 1 Low MODE ON"(EMS=microcurrent)

When the Yellow light is still (not blinking): "EMS 2 Medium Mode ON"

When the Red light is still (not blinking): "EMS 3 High Mode ON"
